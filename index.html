The Residence

Where Heritage Meets Hamptons Living

Built in 1920 and thoughtfully reimagined, this quintessential Hamptons cottage on East Gate Road offers a rare blend of period charm and contemporary luxury. Step through the screened porch into an expansive den with soaring vaulted ceilings, where an oversized wood-burning fireplace anchors a living space flooded with natural light from skylights and French doors.

The first floor flows effortlessly from a formal dining room with a farmhouse table to a designer kitchen featuring custom cabinetry, marble countertops, and a center island with bar seating. Upstairs, the primary suite and two additional guest rooms provide serene retreats with curated wallpapers and bespoke furnishings. Outside, a heated gunite pool, stone patio, and charming red pool house set the stage for summer entertaining on nearly half an acre of private, tree-lined grounds.

Getting Here

Your Journey East

From Manhattan

Approximately 100 miles east via the Long Island Expressway to Route 27. Allow 2–2.5 hours depending on traffic; plan for 3+ hours on summer Fridays.

By Train

LIRR from Penn Station to East Hampton station (approx. 2.5 hours). Direct service runs more frequently during summer months. Station is 4 miles from the property.

By Air

East Hampton Airport (HTO) is just 2 miles away, with private charter and blade helicopter services from Manhattan taking as little as 40 minutes.

By Jitney

The Hampton Jitney offers luxury coach service from Manhattan's Upper East Side directly to Wainscott, with Wi-Fi and refreshments for a comfortable 2.5-hour ride.
